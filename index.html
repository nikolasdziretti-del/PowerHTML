What you can build

Not twenty bullet-point slides — presentations that respond.

🎯

Interactive video

Pause + notes, single/multiple choice, numeric answers with tolerance, fill-in-the-blanks, branching by timecode, and a score screen — all offline.

✂️

Video editor

Trim, cut segments, change speed, and bleep-out audio right inside your slides.

🧪

Simulations & charts

Physics simulations, charts from CSV, QR codes, and KaTeX formulas as first-class slide elements.

🎤

Presenter tools

Presenter view, draw over slides, timers, a name wheel, dice — and yes, confetti.

🎨

Design

Themes, slide transitions, object animations, and reusable templates.

🔌

Truly offline

Everything is embedded in the file. Nothing is uploaded, nothing phones home, nothing expires.

How it works

Three steps, no build tools.

Open

Open PowerHTML_EN.html in any modern browser. That file is the editor.

Build

Add slides, media, questions, and simulations — visually, by mouse.

Save & share

Hit Save and get a standalone show file that opens anywhere — USB stick, email, offline.

Made by a physics teacher, not a programmer — vibecoded over several months of evenings. It works, but it's a big single file, so bug reports are welcome.
